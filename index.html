Project Section

Recipe Hub

The Recipe Hub is an application designed for individuals who enjoy experimenting with different types of meals and cocktails. It utilizes data from an API to generate a randomly selected recipe, providing users with a new and exciting culinary experience. Users also have the option to save their favorite recipes in local storage for easy access and future reference. This application caters to those who value variety in their meals and are always looking to try something new.
